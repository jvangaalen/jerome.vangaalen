About Me

Bio: I am a family man to a beautiful wife and two wonderful daughters. I started out my career going to culinary school and worked my way up through the ranks to rise to the level of Executive Chef! Over the past couple of years I have found the culinary world to be a lot different than when I started. I found that many of the hard working and qualified people that worked for me in kitchens were people with degrees in computer sciences or IT. As the economy got back on its feet, all these people went back to work in the fields of their study. My interest was piqued, and as I researched coding I realized it was something I would like to pursue. I started studying software development with Code Louisville, a coding boot camp. I completed lessons through Treehouse, an online coding school. I enjoy reading tech blogs and following other tech people through twitter and LinkedIn to improve my skills. My next step is to start working and learning even more with a company to make this industry my new career.
